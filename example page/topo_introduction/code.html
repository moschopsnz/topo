The Biography

Not just a pet,
a roommate.

Topo is a Standard Grey chinchilla with a personality that defies the typical "rodent" stereotype. He values personal space, structural routine, and high-quality dust baths.

Unlike dogs that demand affection, Topo earns it. He is an observer—watching from his highest ledge, judging your choice of TV show, and occasionally gracing you with a gentle nose boop when the mood strikes him.

Climate Control

Topo requires an environment strictly under 75°F (24°C). Humidity must be kept low. He is a creature of the dry Andes.

nightlight
Nocturnal Activity

His day begins when yours ends. Expect the soothing sounds of wooden toys being deconstructed at 2 AM.

nutrition
Strict Diet

Timothy hay, high-quality pellets, and filtered water. No fruits, nuts, or seeds. He is a health purist.
